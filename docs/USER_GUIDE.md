# User Guide

## Getting Started

### Connecting Your Wallet

1. Click "Connect Wallet" button
2. Select your wallet provider
3. Approve the connection

### Viewing Your Profile

1. Navigate to your profile page
2. View your builder score, credentials, and skills
3. Edit your profile information

### Searching for Builders

1. Use the search bar on the homepage
2. Filter by skills, credentials, or score
3. View detailed profiles

### Leaderboard

1. Navigate to the Leaderboard page
2. View top builders by score
3. Filter by category (daily, weekly, monthly)

### Achievements

Unlock achievements by:
- Creating your profile
- Reaching score milestones
- Engaging with the community

## Troubleshooting

### Wallet Won't Connect

- Ensure your wallet extension is installed
- Refresh the page and try again
- Check network connection

### Score Not Updating

- Scores update every 24 hours
- Contact support if issues persist
